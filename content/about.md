<h2>Who We Are</h2>
<p><strong>Dependability Holdings LLC</strong> is a private investment research publication. We are not a hedge fund, not a registered investment advisor, and not a broker-dealer. We are an independent research operation focused on one goal: making institutional-quality market analysis available to everyday investors who want to manage their own money intelligently.</p>
<!-- AdSense Vertical -->
<!-- AdSense -->
<p>Our work centers on U.S. equity markets — specifically the S&P 500, the VIX volatility index, and the options strategies that experienced investors use to manage risk and generate income. We publish daily market commentary, weekly recaps, and in-depth educational content.</p>
</div>
<div class="content-block">
<h2>Our Mission</h2>
<p>Making institutional-grade market research accessible to self-directed investors. Wall Street has armies of analysts, Bloomberg terminals, and proprietary data feeds. Individual investors deserve the same rigor applied to their decisions — just presented clearly, without jargon for its own sake.</p>
</div>
<div class="content-block">
<h2>What We Cover</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>S&P 500 Outlooks</strong> — Directional bias, key technical levels, and macro context</li>
<li><strong>VIX &amp; Volatility Analysis</strong> — Volatility regime classification and what it means for strategy selection</li>
<li><strong>Options Strategy Education</strong> — Defined-risk spreads, income strategies, and probability-based trade construction</li>
<li><strong>Risk Management</strong> — Position sizing, portfolio construction, and capital preservation principles</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="content-block">
<h2>Our Approach</h2>
<p>We work with publicly available market data: price and volume from major indices, options chain data, VIX levels, and standard options pricing theory. No proprietary data feeds, no inside information, no algorithmic trading. Our analysis is built on what any informed investor can verify themselves.</p>
<p>We favor defined-risk option strategies — spreads that cap your maximum loss before you place the trade. We do not recommend naked short volatility positions or unlimited-risk strategies. The goal is sustainable income and risk management, not speculation.</p>
</div>
<div class="content-block">
<h2>Editorial Standards</h2>
<p>Every piece of market commentary or educational content we publish goes through a review process before it goes live. We cite our sources — primarily publicly available data from Yahoo Finance, the CBOE, FRED, and SEC filings. We correct errors promptly and update market commentary when conditions change meaningfully.</p>
<p>We are transparent about what we know and what we don't. Market forecasts are inherently uncertain. We present our reasoning, our framework, and our level of confidence — and we don't pretend certainty we don't have.</p>
</div>
<div class="content-block">
<h2>Not a Registered Investment Advisor</h2>
<p><strong>Dependability Holdings LLC is not registered with the SEC or any state as an investment advisor.</strong> We are an educational publication. Nothing on this website constitutes personalized investment advice, a recommendation to buy or sell any security, or a solicitation to open a brokerage account.</p>
<p>All content is for informational and educational purposes only. Options trading involves significant risk. Please consult a qualified financial advisor before making any investment decisions.</p>
