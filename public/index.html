Who This Research Is For

Dependability Holdings publishes free market research for self-directed investors who manage their own portfolios — particularly those using options strategies such as covered calls, cash-secured puts, iron condors, and credit spreads. Our audience ranges from experienced traders to those just learning how calls and puts work.

We are not a signal service. We do not promise win rates or guarantee returns. What we offer is a consistent, clearly-reasoned set of market observations and trade ideas presented with full risk disclosure.
